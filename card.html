leopard tortoise

Leopard tortoise had yellow, yellowish-brown or reddish carapace (upper part of the shell) with black blotches and spots that resemble the spotted pattern of leopard's fur (hence the name).

  • Scientific Name: Geochelone pardalis
  • Weight: 40 to 120 pounds
  • Lifespan: 80 to 100 years
  • Habitat: the coastal plains and semi-arid grasslands and scrublands

Leopard tortoise is type of land-dwelling reptile and the fourth largest species of tortoise in the world. Together with rhino beetle, red-billed buffalo weaver, elephant shrew and ant lion, leopard tortoise is a member of the "Small five"
